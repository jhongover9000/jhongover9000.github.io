moving on...

looking back...


the brief

30 minutes to film, then 30 minutes to edit. no designated topic, no preplanned style. on the spot. the film was a project for NYUAD Communications Lab.



the concept

among the videos collected, a number were taken whilst moving in a certain direction. this ultimately led to a theme that embraced the idea of moving forward; advancing, regardless of whatever comes one's way. whatever comes in life–– hardships, trials, or obstacles ––to push on and come out on the other end, stronger and more experienced than before.

pressing forward...


the process

based on the theme, we selected several videos and aligned them in a chronological fashion in terms of daylight. in addition, we decided to choose the song 'Don't Stop Me Now' as the background music for our film, as it went along with the theme of moving forward and not stopping.

to infinity...


the design

this website takes the concept of moving forward and applies it in various forms. the scrolling direction, as well as the direction in which this site progresses is in relation to the theme of progressing forward.
