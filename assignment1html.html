moving on.

looking back,


the brief

30 minutes to film, then 30 minutes to edit. no designated topic, no preplanned style. on the spot.



the concept

among the videos collected, a number were taken whilst moving in a certain direction. this ultimately led to a theme that embraced the idea of moving forward; advancing, regardless of whatever comes one's way. whatever comes in life–– hardships, trials, or obstacles ––to push on and come out on the other end, stronger and more experienced than before.

pressing forward;

the design

this website takes the concept of moving forward and applies it in various forms. the scrolling direction, as well as the direction in which this site progresses is in relation to the theme of progressing forward. also, the navigation constellation below is meant to add more meaning to the idea of advancing: that with each moment we live, we leave behind us a trail of legacies. at the end, we realize what we truly gained from each experience and see how they all interacted with one another, forming a coherent picture of linked points in time–– a constellation of stars, shining bright in the night sky.

and beyond.
