An Interactive Experience.

This website is a mix between video and interaction, meshed together to create a story that follows the decisions you make.

Different decisions can lead to different responses. Watch as your interactions change the content of the video.

Multiple Choices, Multiple Endings.

At certain points during the video, decisions will appear in the form of two choices at the bottom of the screen. Depending on your decision, the outcome of the story may change.

Different decisions can lead to different endings. Try to find all of them through multiple runs of the video.

Something's Wrong... I Can Feel It.

There's a special feature implemented in the video to keep it from being too easy. Discover it, then use it to get the best ending possible. Or use it to your advantage to purposefully change the flow of the story.
