sound. or not to sound.

So. This was one of those ambitious projects. Note: don't play multiple audio files at once. The sound gets super distorted. Honestly, I'm not sure if it has to do with the memory of the browser. In truth, we still don't actually know what was (and still is) causing the issues.

Why was this ambitious? Well, the audio for one, but also the concept. We wanted to play some six or seven audio files all at once and have visuals linked to each of those. Turns out that it doesn't work. The limit for no distortion seems to be two files. Now, if we had known this, we would have done something else to make up for this. But we didn't, since apparently nobody (was stupid enough to have) tried this before. Or maybe they did it in a way that actually worked.

The thing is that it does work. It's just really a hit or miss. With the visuals, though, it's almost always a miss. The visuals (and audio) are played via p5.js, which may or may not be part of the problem. We simply don't know. This project was interesting, but very time consuming, resulting in multiple all-nighters in trying to get it to work. Oh, we also kinda did this in a few days' time, so that also added to the problem. If you want the full documentation, you can find it here.
