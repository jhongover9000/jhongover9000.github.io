humble(?) beginnings.

Aight, so this was the first time that I tried HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. And me being me, I decided that it would be a great idea to try something that seemed like a reach. A horizontal-scrolling website. Whoopee.

Turns out that it's actually a lot more difficult than just "make everything scroll this way". In any case, maybe half of my worktime was spent trying to figure out how to make it work. At one point, I rewrote the way that I had initially designed the horizontal aspect of the site because I realized it wasn't working with all of the elements within the site.

As a result, the website itself is rather basic, with only a few little experimental features alongside the horizontal scrolling part of it. An example is the dynamically changing background... wait is that it? Anyway, it was an interesting first assignment that didn't go as well as I wanted it to, but became the start of a trend of one ambitious project after another. Not much else to say about this.
