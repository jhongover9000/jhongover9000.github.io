Hey, Evelyn.

You’ve already guessed it’s me by my handwriting, haven’t you? This is what I get for being your best friend for twelve years.

Anyway… there’s something that I want to say to you, something I’ve wanted to tell you for the longest time.

It’s taken all the courage I have just to write this down, so promise me you won’t laugh when you read this.

Here it goes.

I think I’m in love with you.

I know how much our relationship means to you–– it means the world to me, too ––so I won’t push you for an answer. I won’t even ask you if you read this or not.

I’ll just be there for you, like I always have.

When you’re happy, I’ll be the first to laugh along–– even if it’s at one of your terrible jokes.

And when you’re sad...

I’ll be a shoulder to cry on.

You dream big, Evelyn.

I know you won’t settle for the norm, so one day you’ll leave.

But no matter how far apart we might be,

even if it means waking up at 3 in the morning,

I’ll listen to whatever you have to say.

And then we’ll be together again, just like that.

Maybe it won’t be for a while, but I know that we’ll see each other. I'm sure of it.

And wherever that might be, whatever we might be doing...

I promise I’ll follow you with a smile on my face.

And I don’t care if it takes days..

months..

or even years.

I’ll continue to be your closest friend.

The one person you can tell everything.

No matter what this life throws at me, I’ll bear it all gladly.

Waiting, hoping, praying that one day, my feelings will finally reach you.

And when that day comes...

Will you say yes?

But take your time, Evelyn.

There’s no need to rush to an answer.

Regardless of what happens, I’ll still be here for you.

So know that I’ll be yours, Evelyn…

Yours till the end.

a love letter.
